## 📝 Ingredients

- 240 g of flour 🌾
- 75 g of powdered sugar 🍚
- 50 g of brown sugar 🍬
- 25 g of almond powder 🌰
- 1 teaspoon of salt 🧂
- 125 g of butter 🧈
- 1 egg 🥚

## 👩‍🍳 Steps

1. **Add** :
   - 240 g of flour 🌾
   - 75 g of powdered sugar 🍚
   - 50 g of brown sugar 🍬
   - 25 g of almond powder 🌰
   - 1 teaspoon of salt 🧂

   **Mix** 🌀

2. **Add** :
   - 125 g of butter 🧈
   - 1 egg 🥚

   **Mix** 🌀

3. **Chill** :
   - Let the dough rest for 2 hours in the refrigerator 🕒❄️
